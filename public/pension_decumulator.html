Assumptions used

Inflation- the calculator allows for annual investment growth of 2%, 5% or 8%. Inflation is assumed to be 2.5% if investment growth is 5%. For 2% growth, inflation is 0.5% and for 8% growth it is 4.5%. These assumptions are set down by the Financial Conduct Authority for pension projections. The default setting is 5% and can be changed in advanced options.
